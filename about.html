About Us

At BeyondTheDegree, we are a passionate group of researchers, educators, and industry professionals dedicated to understanding the real challenges students face when transitioning from campus to the corporate world.

We believe that education should empower individuals to achieve their career goals seamlessly. However, a significant gap exists between academic learning and industry expectations. Our mission is to study this gap deeply and develop insights that can help bridge it.

Through our comprehensive surveys and data analysis, we aim to map out the exact hurdles students encounter across various domains. This initiative is our first step towards building a future where every student has the right tools, knowledge, and guidance to succeed.
